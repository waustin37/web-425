BRAIN ENO



Eno in 2015 Interview for "How We Get To Next"


Brian Eno, a pioneering English musician, producer, and visual artist, reshaped the landscape of music. Born in 1948, he gained fame as a member of Roxy Music before embarking on a trailblazing solo career. Eno's ambient soundscapes and innovative production techniques, evident in albums like "Music for Airports," inspired countless artists. His eclectic collaborations span from U2 to David Bowie. Eno's imprint on modern music and his artistic endeavors continue to captivate and influence worldwide.
